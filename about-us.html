About Us

Welcome to Starbucks Philippines, your home away from home. We are a proud member of the globally recognized Starbucks Corporation, which has been serving premium coffee since 1971. Our journey in the Philippines began in 1997, when we opened our first store at 6750 Ayala Avenue, Makati City. Since then, we have grown to over 300 stores across the country, becoming a beloved fixture in the local coffee culture.

At Starbucks Philippines, we are more than just a coffee shop. We are a community. We are a hub for conversation and meeting, a part of the daily routine, and we couldn't be happier about it. Our mission is to inspire and nurture the human spirit – one person, one cup, and one neighborhood at a time.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source the finest and freshest ingredients from around the globe, ensuring that every cup of coffee, tea, and every bite of food you enjoy with us is of the highest quality. Our baristas are trained to craft each beverage with care and precision, ensuring a unique and personalized experience every time you visit.

Our History

Starbucks Philippines is a subsidiary of Starbucks Corporation, a global brand known for its exceptional coffee and inviting café atmosphere. We entered the Philippine market through a licensing agreement with Rustan Coffee Corporation, and our first store opened in Makati City.

Over the years, we have expanded and now proudly operate over 500 stores nationwide, employing thousands of dedicated partners (employees) who share our love for coffee culture and customer service.

What sets us apart from the competition is our unwavering focus on quality, community, and sustainability. We source the finest coffee beans from around the world, meticulously roasting them to perfection to bring out their unique flavors. Additionally, we are dedicated to establishing strong relationships with local coffee farmers, ensuring fair trade and responsible sourcing practices.

To inspire and nurture the unique and precious human spirit, awakening the depths of joy, fulfillment, and connection – uplifting one person, one meticulously crafted cup of coffee, and fostering a sense of belonging, unity, and empowerment within each vibrant neighborhood we serve – passionately transforming lives and breathing life into remarkable experiences, one profound interaction at a time.

To be the premier purveyor of the finest coffee in the world while maintaining our uncompromising principles. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and inspired.
